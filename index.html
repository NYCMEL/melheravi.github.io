About Me

I am an experienced UX Designer, POC Builder, and Developer with over 9 years of expertise across finance, banking, and government sectors. I specialize in UI/UX design, front-end development, prototyping, and AI-driven UX innovation. I have led UX strategy and design initiatives for organizations such as JPMorgan Chase, KPMG, Barclays, Citi, Fidelity Investments, Fiserv and others focusing on improving usability in financial platforms and AI-powered enterprise applications. My skills include responsive design, accessibility (WCAG), usability testing, and interaction design. I am proficient in Figma, Sketch, Axure, and Miro, with a strong emphasis on UX frameworks, component libraries, and creating cohesive, user-centered digital experiences.
